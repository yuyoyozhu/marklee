Career

In April 2016, Mark was confirmed by SM Enteratinment to debut along 5 other members of SM Rookies: Taeil, Taeyong, Doyoung, Ten, and Jaehyun in NCT's first subunit, NCT U. On April 9th, they released their debut song titled "The 7th Sense". SM announced NCT's second subunit, NCT 127, on July 1, consisting of 7 members (later, 3 more members were added). Their debut mini-album, NCT#127, released on July 10th, 2016. Mark debuted again in NCT's other subunit, NCT Dream on August 24th with their first single, "Chewing Gum". Mark also participated in NCT 2018 Empathy, NCT's first studio album. In August 2019, Mark Lee debuted once again in SM Entertainment's collaboritave boy group SuperM with their debut single titled "Jopping".
