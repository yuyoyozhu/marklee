Background

Mark Lee was born on August 2, 1999 in Toronto, Ontario. He moved to New York around 2004/2005 and graduated elementary school in 2010. Shortly after, he moved to Vancouver. In 2012 he participated in the SM Global Audition and passed, moving to South Korea later that year where he joined SM Entertainment and recieved training. In Korea, he continued to recieve middle school education, and graduated from Eonju Middle School in 2015. He went on to attend the School of Performing Arts Seoul (SOPA) for high school while preparing for his debut.
